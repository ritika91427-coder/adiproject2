About the Department

NABL-Certified Diagnostic Excellence

Wangduk Health's Diagnostics Department is a NABL-certified facility offering comprehensive pathology, biochemistry, microbiology, and haematology services. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with fully automated analysers, ensuring consistent, accurate results that physicians and patients can rely on.

With a turnaround time designed to support rapid clinical decisions, our lab operates from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily, with emergency services available around the clock. Our quality control protocols and trained laboratory scientists ensure that every test result meets the highest standards of precision.

From routine blood panels to complex molecular diagnostics, we cover the full spectrum of laboratory testing — empowering your healthcare team to make well-informed, evidence-based treatment decisions quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Diagnostics FAQs

Our diagnostic lab is open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Emergency and critical tests are processed 24×7. We recommend coming early in the morning for fasting tests such as blood glucose and lipid profiles.
Most routine tests like CBC, glucose, and lipid profile can be done without a prescription. However, for specialised tests like biopsies, molecular diagnostics, or complex hormone panels, a doctor's referral is recommended to ensure the right tests are ordered and results are interpreted correctly.
Most routine blood tests are ready within 4–6 hours. Culture tests may take 3–5 days. Biopsy and histopathology reports typically take 3–7 working days. Emergency tests are processed on priority within 1–2 hours. Reports are available digitally and at the lab counter.
Fasting (8–12 hours) is required for tests like fasting blood sugar, HbA1c, lipid profile, and some liver function tests. CBC, thyroid, urine analysis, and most other tests do not require fasting. Please check with our lab reception when booking your test.
Yes, our laboratory is NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories) certified. This certification ensures that our testing processes, equipment, and quality standards meet national and international benchmarks for accuracy and reliability.
