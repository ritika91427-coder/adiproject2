About Wangduk Health and Research

Established in 1889, this is one of the best regional hospitals, providing 24×7 healthcare services. The hospital is equipped with modern medical technologies such as MRI and CT scan facilities. Adequate bed availability ensures that patients receive beds without long waiting times, with paperwork completed afterward for convenience.

The hospital has a green and clean environment with well-maintained hygiene standards, including clean toilets. It is supported by more than 145 active staff members dedicated to patient care. A food counter is available inside the hospital campus, and all essential facilities are provided to ensure comfort and quality healthcare services.

Director's Message

Prof. Laslong Wangduk

Cardiology Orthopedics Yoshida Award AIIMS Surat

Prof. Wangduk served as the Dean of a leading hospital in Surat and was honored with the prestigious Yoshida Award for his outstanding contributions to healthcare.

He completed his medical education at AIIMS Surat before pursuing advanced training abroad, where he worked for two years, gaining valuable international experience.

He specializes in Cardiology and Orthopedics and is known for his expertise, dedication, and commitment to patient care.
