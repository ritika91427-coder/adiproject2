About the Department

Precision Care for Bones & Joints

The Orthopedics Department at Wangduk Health and Research provides comprehensive musculoskeletal care for patients of all ages. Led by Prof. Laslong Wangduk, our team combines deep surgical expertise with advanced rehabilitation protocols to restore full function after injury, disease, or degeneration.

We specialise in joint replacement surgeries, arthroscopic procedures, spinal interventions, and sports injury management. Our state-of-the-art operation theatres are equipped with navigation-assisted surgery systems, ensuring millimetre-level precision in every procedure.

Beyond surgery, our holistic approach includes physiotherapy, pain management, and occupational therapy — ensuring patients regain independence and quality of life as quickly and safely as possible.

Orthopedics FAQs
